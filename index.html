About the Conference

The Conference is an annual event and the largest gathering of banking and finance professionals in Africa, with an estimated audience of 10,000 comprising professionals, policymakers, regulators, members of the academia, operators, and other stakeholders in the financial services industry. The objective of the Conference is to, among others, provide a veritable platform for subject experts and industry stakeholders to drive conversations and unanimously design a clear road map towards repositioning the Financial Services Industry for economic growth and stability.
